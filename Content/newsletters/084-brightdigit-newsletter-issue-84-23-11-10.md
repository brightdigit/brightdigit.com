Hello everyone!

I’m thrilled to announce **the launch of the BrightDigit Patreon page**,
and I want you to be a part of it! As a valued member and fan, your
support will not only enhance the content but also provide you with
fantastic perks:

1.  **Early Access:** Get a head start on our podcasts, videos,
    articles, and exclusive coding materials.

2.  **Content Contribution:** Have a say in the content creation process
    by suggesting questions for our guests.

3.  **Behind-the-Scenes Access:** Sneak a peek at our recording schedule
    and be in the know about what's coming up.

4.  **Exclusive Community:** Gain entry into our fans' Slack channel,
    connecting with like-minded supporters.

Your support means the world to me If you've enjoyed our work, consider
joining us on this exciting journey.
