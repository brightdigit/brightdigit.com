Hello everyone,

  

Besides the new Vision Pro and visionOS, big OS updates came to watchOS.
<a href="https://brightdigit.com/articles/watchos-10/"
target="_blank"><strong>watchOS 10 could fundamentally change how we
make apps for the Apple Watch</strong></a>**, becoming a platform where
you can build dedicated apps.** If you know me, you know I’m a big fan
of the what the Apple Watch *can do*.
<a href="https://brightdigit.com/articles/watchos-10/"
target="_blank">In the latest article we tackle the big updates coming
to watchOS 10 this fall.</a>

<a href="https://youtu.be/QL2xpDqBGxo" style="display:block"
target="_blank" data-block-id="53"><img
src="https://dim.mcusercontent.com/cs/cb3bba007ed171091f55c47f0/video_thumbnails_new/f3d6d4f05576bcca0e7eca8bd6a8b974.png?w=564&amp;dpr=2"
style="border:0;width:564px;height:auto;max-width:100%;display:block"
width="564"
alt="Arm Sling for Apple Watch Developers with Hidde van der Ploeg" /></a>

We cover some great new features such as:

-   Full Navigation

-   Integrated Smart Stack

-   Improved Debugging Experience for Developers

and more…

<a href="https://brightdigit.com/articles/watchos-10/"
style="display:block" target="_blank" data-block-id="51"><img
src="https://dim.mcusercontent.com/cs/cb3bba007ed171091f55c47f0/images/917f9151-9e2a-bbe3-6cd5-7e8e9018edb5.jpeg?w=564&amp;dpr=2"
style="border:0;width:660px;height:auto;max-width:100%;border-radius:16px;display:block"
width="660" alt="watchOS 10" /></a>

**watchOS 10 is a maturity milestone for the Apple Watch.** If you’ve
been on the fence about building a Watch app –
<a href="https://brightdigit.com/articles/watchos-10/"
target="_blank">now is the time to reevaluate.</a>

Leo
