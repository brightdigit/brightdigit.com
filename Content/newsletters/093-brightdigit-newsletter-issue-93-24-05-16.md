Hello everyone!

I had a great time at Deep Dish Swift. It was a fantastic time to
connect with fellow Swift developers and learn from the best in the
community. I especially enjoyed:

-   Matt Massicotte on Swift Proposals

-   "Grand Gestures in SwiftUI" with Jessie Linden

-   "The Temporal Axis of Space-Time" with Dave DeLong

-   "Automation for App Localization" with Chris Vasselli

-   "Diving Deep into Sendable" with Tim Condon

-   "The Simple Ingredients of a SwiftData App" with Daniel Steinberg

I'm already looking forward to
<a href="https://deepdishswift.com/" target="_blank">next year's event
in 2025</a>.

## <a href="https://github.com/brightdigit/AviaryInsights"
target="_blank"><strong>AviaryInsights: Analytics with
Plausible</strong></a>

I am thrilled to announce the release of AviaryInsights, a Swift package
designed to integrate analytics using Plausible. This tool simplifies
the process of gathering insights, making it a valuable asset for
developers focused on privacy-friendly analytics. Check it out
[here](https://github.com/brightdigit/AviaryInsights).

## <a href="https://github.com/brightdigit/FeatherQuill"
target="_blank"><strong>FeatherQuill: Feature Flags Without
Backend</strong></a>

Introducing FeatherQuill, our new Swift package that enables feature
flags without the need for a backend. This package is perfect for
managing features and improving your development workflow. Discover more
[here](https://github.com/brightdigit/FeatherQuill).
